Margaritas Anyone?

Whether you like them on the rocks or frozen, we have the Top 10 margaritas in Austin!

Matt's El Rancho

Come for the enchiladas, stay for your Uber after drinking one of their potent margaritas!

Trudy's

With funky flavors like sangria and ruby red, Trudy's is a must! Try them on a Tuesday after work for a top shelf for the same price as a regular. What a steal!

Gabriela's Downtown

Known for its strawberry margarita, smoothly blended and poured inside of a hollowed out pineapple, Gabriela's Downtown is one of the hottest spots for a margarita in central Austin.

Fresa's

What's better than a tall glass of water on a hot Texas day? A cucumber cilantro margarita from Fresa's. If you're more adventurous, try their prickly pear margarita, with a chile sugar rim, of course!

Azul Tequila

Ever heard of exotic margaritas? Once you try a chamoy flavored margarita from Azul Tequila, you will be able to answer "yes" to that question!

Vivo

Tequila + lime juice + flowers = most asthetically pleasing margarita ever. They also taste pretty great!
